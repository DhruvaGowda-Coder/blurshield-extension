Core Features
Everything You Need to Stay Safe
From auto-detection to meeting protection — BlurShield covers every angle.
🔍
Auto-Detection Engine
55 regex patterns detect API keys, tokens, passwords, and PII from AWS, GitHub, Stripe, OpenAI, Google Cloud, and 25+ more services in real-time.
🔒
Instant Blur Protection
Secrets are automatically blurred with a customizable Gaussian blur. Double-click or hover to temporarily reveal individual items.
🎥
Pre-Meeting Scan
Automatically scans all open tabs before a Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams call starts. Shows exactly which tabs have exposed secrets.
🖱️
Custom Blur Zones
Click any element on any page to manually blur it. Perfect for dashboards, balances, personal info, or anything the auto-scanner can't catch.
📋
Clipboard Guard
Warns you when you copy sensitive data and again when you paste it — with options to Allow, Redact, or Cancel.
🔴
Leak Preview Mode
Highlights all detected secrets with red outlines instead of blurring — perfect for auditing a page before a meeting.
🎬
Meeting Mode
One shortcut to blur ALL text content on the entire page instantly. Perfect for when someone unexpectedly asks to see your screen.
📸
Blurred Screenshots
Capture a screenshot of the current tab with all blurs intact. Download as PNG — safe to share with your team.
🛡️
Screen-Share Intercept PRO
Intercepts your browser's screen-share API (getDisplayMedia) to automatically run a full security scan before any recording begins. Works with Zoom, Meet, Teams, Loom, OBS, and more.
📊
Security Score PRO
Real-time security score based on the severity and count of detected secrets on the current page. See Critical, High, and Medium severity badges.
📈
Stats Dashboard PRO
7-day activity chart showing secrets blurred, times revealed, active days, and custom zones saved. Track your protection over time.
🌐
Domain Whitelisting
Whitelist trusted domains where you don't need protection — like internal tools, dev environments, or known-safe sites.
🔤
Custom Patterns PRO
Add your own regex patterns to detect company-specific secrets, internal project IDs, or any custom sensitive data format.
🔔
Recording Banner PRO
When screen recording is detected, a banner appears confirming BlurShield is active and how many secrets are protected.
⌨️
Custom Keyboard Shortcuts
Reassign every shortcut to your preferred key combo. Changes apply instantly to all open tabs.
